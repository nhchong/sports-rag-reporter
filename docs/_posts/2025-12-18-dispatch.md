## The Week in Focus
Don Cherry's solidified their top rank with a commanding win, extending their lead. The Muffin Men leveraged their matchup for crucial points, while the 4 Lines and Flat-Earthers' deadlock kept the mid-table scramble heated. Official presence was varied, shaping game flows under Toronto's chilly December skies.

## Game Night Breakdown
Monday, December 15th, brought intense action. Don Cherry's demolished The Shockers 6-1 at UCC Arena, with Ryan Willms (2G, 2A) and Koch Tsatsos (1G, 3A) leading the charge. Referees Mark Donohoe and Basil Jones were active, calling four Shockers penalties that effectively curtailed their offense.
> Don Cherry's: 6 goals, The Shockers: 1 goal.

At St. Mikes, the Muffin Men secured a 5-1 victory over The Sahara. Owen Burry (2G) and Cosimo Morin (2G) powered their decisive performance. The night's most dramatic contest saw 4 Lines and the Flat-Earthers fight to a thrilling 4-4 tie. Jamie Poole (2G) netted for 4 Lines, but Anthony Pennino (2G, 1PPG) buried a crucial late powerplay equalizer for the Flat-Earthers. Referees John Hall and Shane Bowler handled multiple infractions across both St. Mikes games.
> Muffin Men: 5 goals, The Sahara: 1 goal.
> 4 Lines: 4 goals, Flat-Earthers: 4 goals (1PPG).

Closing the week on a brisk Thursday, December 18th, 4 Lines took a 4-2 win over Pdiym at the Mattamy Athletic Centre, with Thomas Bevilacqua contributing. Referee Sonny Martinello maintained a notably hands-off approach, calling just one minor penalty all game, making for a remarkably free-flowing contest.
> 4 Lines: 4 goals. Pdiym: 2 goals.

## The Three Stars
*   **1st Star: Michael Murphy (Don Cherry's)** – His league-leading 29 points (16G, 13A) underscored Don Cherry's dominant offensive output.
*   **2nd Star: Charles Levy (Muffin Men)** – A potent 27 points (17G, 10A) fueled the Muffin Men's critical win.
*   **3rd Star: Anthony Pennino (Flat-Earthers)** – With 23 points (13G, 10A) and a clutch powerplay goal, he salvaged a vital tie.
