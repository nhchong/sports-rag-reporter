## Standings Jousting and Hot Streaks

The frosty grip of early December in Toronto brought a heated week of DMHL action. Don Cherry's reaffirmed their top spot, while The Sahara and The Shockers made significant moves up the standings ladder.

## This Week's Action

**Monday, December 1st (St. Mikes Arena):** The Sahara edged out 4 Lines 6-4. Isaac Jeffers (1G, 2A) and Derrick Wong (1G, 2A, including a pivotal PP goal) orchestrated a third-period comeback. Sam Goldstein's two goals sealed it, overcoming two shorthanded tallies from 4 Lines. Referees Carlos Verde and Mike Bonke had a busy night, calling five penalties in total.

**Tuesday, December 2nd (UCC Arena):** Don Cherry's secured a 4-2 victory against the Flat-Earthers. Sean Murphy's two goals, including a crucial shorthanded marker, proved the difference, solidifying Don Cherry's league-leading
> "Goals For: 85, GFA: 4.47."
The late 10:30 PM tilt saw minimal penalties, just three minors called.

**Wednesday, December 3rd (St. Mikes Arena):** The Shockers dominated the Muffin Men with a commanding 6-1 win. Caden Bower (1G, 1A) contributed to a four-goal second period, overwhelming the Muffin Men whose lone goal came late from Charles Levy. This victory propels The Shockers into a points tie with The Sahara, both now ahead of Muffin Men.

## The Low B Dispatch Three Stars of the Week

**1st Star:** Isaac Jeffers (The Sahara) – 1 Goal, 2 Assists
**2nd Star:** Derrick Wong (The Sahara) – 1 Goal (PPG), 2 Assists
**3rd Star:** Sean Murphy (Don Cherry's) – 2 Goals (1 SHG)
