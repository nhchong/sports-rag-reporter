### Cherry's Charge Ahead; Lucky Loser Race Tightens in Low B Playoffs
*Don Cherry's secures Round 2 berth with dominant play, while the fight for the single 'Lucky Loser' spot intensifies based on goal differential after pivotal Game 1 matchups.*

The opening slate of Monday/Wednesday Low B playoffs at St. Mikes Arena set a clear path for one team while leaving the others in a tense "Race to Three" for advancement.

**Round 1: Series Status**

*   **Don Cherry's (1st Seed) vs. Muffin Men (6th Seed)**: Don Cherry's delivered a commanding offensive performance, securing a 6-0 victory. Michael Murphy led the charge with two goals and an assist, supported by Sean Murphy's two goals and Cassady Louks' goal and assist. Ryan Willms and Colin Digiacomo each contributed two assists. This decisive win gives Don Cherry's a full 4 points in their series, clinching their spot in Round 2. The Muffin Men, now at 0 points, are eliminated from their series.
*   **The Sahara (2nd Seed) vs. 4 Lines (5th Seed)**: This contest was a tightly contested affair, concluding in a 1-1 tie. 4 Lines' Adam Miller broke the deadlock late in the third, only for Kosta Likourezos of The Sahara to equalize shortly after. The Sahara now holds a 3-1 series lead, needing just a tie in Game 2 to advance.
*   **The Shockers (3rd Seed) vs. Flat-Earthers (4th Seed)**: The Shockers edged out the Flat-Earthers with a 2-1 victory. Flat-Earthers' Reid Chessell capitalized on a power play in the second period for their lone marker. However, The Shockers responded in the third period with even-strength goals from Keelan Hughes, followed by Patrick Mcmullan's game-winner. The Shockers now lead the series 3-1.

**Lucky Loser Projections**

With Don Cherry's already advancing, the focus shifts to the three losing teams for the single 'Lucky Loser' spot in Round 2. Based on current playoff standings:

*   **Flat-Earthers**: 1 Point, -1 Goal Differential
*   **4 Lines**: 1 Point, -2 Goal Differential
*   **Muffin Men**: 0 Points, -10 Goal Differential

Currently, the Flat-Earthers hold a marginal but crucial advantage over 4 Lines due to their superior goal differential, despite both teams sitting at 1 point. The Muffin Men are out of contention for the 'Lucky Loser' spot. Game 2 will be a battle not just for series advancement, but for playoff survival.
