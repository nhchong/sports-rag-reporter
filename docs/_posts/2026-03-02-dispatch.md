**Playoff Predicaments: Favorites Claim Footholds, Middle Seeds Lock Horns**
Early series wins and a crucial tie shape the Round 2 landscape, with goal differential now a looming specter for those vying for the Lucky Loser spot.

**Game Summaries**

*   **Don Cherry's (5) vs Muffin Men (1)**
    The top-seeded Don Cherry's asserted their dominance at St. Mikes Arena, dispatching the Muffin Men with a convincing 5-1 victory. Michael Murphy, Dongyeon Kim, and Sean Murphy each contributed a goal and an assist, showcasing their offensive depth. Don Cherry's kept a clean sheet in the penalty department, while the Muffin Men took two minors, including a holding call on Cosimo Morin that led to a power-play goal by Michael Murphy in the second period. Cosimo Morin notched the Muffin Men's lone tally.

*   **The Sahara (6) vs 4 Lines (4)**
    The Sahara secured a 6-4 win over 4 Lines, also at St. Mikes. A relentless offensive attack saw six different players score for The Sahara, including a goal and an assist from Kosta Likourezos. 4 Lines' Adam Miller put up a valiant effort with two goals, and Shane Ferguson added a shorthanded marker in the third period. 4 Lines faced discipline issues, incurring three minor penalties, including a crucial interference call in the third that led to Ferguson's shorty.

*   **Flat-Earthers (1) vs The Shockers (1)**
    In the evening's tightest contest, Flat-Earthers and The Shockers played to a 1-1 draw at St. Mikes. Marcus Simmonds opened the scoring for the Flat-Earthers in the first period, while Alex Matheson responded for The Shockers shortly after. The officials, Evan Benwell and Brad Kuchar, were kept busy, with Flat-Earthers accumulating 6 PIMs and The Shockers 10 PIMs in a highly competitive affair.

**Series Math: The Race to Three**

*   **Don Cherry's vs Muffin Men:** Don Cherry's holds a commanding 2 points. A tie or win in Game 2 punches their ticket to Round 2. The Muffin Men, with 0 points, must win Game 2 to force overtime.
*   **The Sahara vs 4 Lines:** The Sahara sits at 2 points. They need only 1 more point (a tie or win in Game 2) to advance. 4 Lines, with 0 points, faces a win-or-go-home scenario in Game 2 to push for overtime.
*   **Flat-Earthers vs The Shockers:** This series is deadlocked at 1 point apiece. Game 2 is an outright winner-take-all contest. If tied after regulation, a sudden-death 4-on-4 overtime will ensue, followed by a shootout if necessary.

**The Lucky Loser Projections**
The race for the solitary 'Lucky Loser' spot is already taking shape. Among the teams currently sitting at 0 points, 4 Lines holds a -2 Goal Differential, while the Muffin Men are at -4. Critically, both the Flat-Earthers and The Shockers currently hold 1 point with a 0 Goal Differential. Should either of them lose their series in Game 2 (after regulation in a tied points scenario), they would be in the most advantageous position for the Lucky Loser berth due to their superior points and goal differential compared to the outright Game 1 losers.
