The Gauntlet Thrown: Round 1 Delivers Dominance, Lucky Loser Earns Semi-Final Berth
The DMHL Low B Playoffs have ignited, with Round 1 concluding in a flurry of decisive victories and one narrow escape, setting the stage for a compelling Semi-Final bracket.

## Round 1 Recap: The Victors Emerge

Don Cherry's asserted their top-seed dominance over the Muffin Men, sweeping the series with a commanding 4 points to 0. Their final game showcased a relentless offensive display, securing a 6-0 shutout. Michael Murphy led the charge with two goals (one assisted by Ryan Willms and Cassady Louks, another by Colin Digiacomo and Sam Pasich), while Sean Murphy also buried two (one assisted by Koch Tsatsos, another by Colin Digiacomo). Dongyeon Kim and Cassady Louks also found the back of the net, the latter assisted by Jack Pirie and Michael Murphy. The Muffin Men were unable to generate sustained offense throughout the series, managing only one goal across both contests.

In a tighter battle, The Sahara advanced past 4 Lines, securing 3 points to 1. Their decisive 1-1 tie on Monday solidified their progression. Kosta Likourezos netted The Sahara's lone goal, assisted by Isaac Jeffers, late in the third period. Adam Miller, assisted by Luke Gravett, scored earlier for 4 Lines to keep the game deadlocked. Drew Ingall of The Sahara was assessed a tripping minor in an otherwise disciplined affair.

The Shockers also secured their Semi-Final spot with 3 points, overcoming the Flat-Earthers (1 point) in a hard-fought series. Their most recent encounter, a 2-1 victory, saw the Flat-Earthers, operating with a short bench, fall victim to The Shockers' late-game surge. Flat-Earthers' Reid Chessell capitalized on a power play opportunity in the second period, assisted by Marcus Simmonds and Matthew McIntosh. However, Keelan Hughes, assisted by Levi Tobe, and Patrick Mcmullan, assisted by Josh Pope, scored crucial third-period goals to clinch the win for The Shockers.

## The Lucky Loser Miracle

The Flat-Earthers have miraculously earned a reprieve, securing the sole Lucky Loser spot. Despite their elimination, their playoff goal differential of -1 edged out 4 Lines (-2) and the Muffin Men (-10), granting them a second life.

## Semi-Finals: The Brackets Are Set

The path to the final is now clear. Based on playoff points and goal differential, the Semi-Final matchups are:

### Don Cherry's (1st Seed) vs. Flat-Earthers (Lucky Loser)
The league's regular-season juggernaut, Don Cherry's (30 Pts, +33 GD), faces a Flat-Earthers squad (23 Pts, +1 GD) that fought hard despite being short-handed. Don Cherry's averaged a league-best 4.35 goals per game in the regular season. This could be a high-octane affair, though the Flat-Earthers' ability to draw penalties (79 regular season PIM compared to Don Cherry's 57 PIM) might test Don Cherry's discipline.

### The Sahara (2nd Seed) vs. The Shockers (3rd Seed)
This matchup pits The Sahara (17 Pts, -22 GD regular season) against The Shockers (16 Pts, 0 GD regular season). Regular season meetings often showcased a more physical edge, with The Shockers leading the league in penalty minutes (88 PIM). This promises to be a grind, where special teams and defensive solidity will likely determine the outcome.
