### Semi-Finals Set: Don Cherry's, Sahara, and Shockers Advance

The opening round of the Monday/Wednesday Low B playoffs concluded at St. Mikes Arena, solidifying a Semi-Final bracket that features the league's top three seeds and one resilient survivor.

#### Series Post-Mortem

**Don Cherry's (1) vs Muffin Men (6): The Sweep**
The top-seeded Don Cherry's left no doubt about their championship aspirations, securing a 6-0 shutout on Monday to complete the series sweep. Michael Murphy (2G, 1A) and Sean Murphy (2G) provided the offensive engine, while the defense stifled any attempt at a Muffin Men comeback. Don Cherry's finishes the round with a perfect 4 points.

**The Sahara (2) vs 4 Lines (5): OT Thriller**
This was the most competitive series of the week. After a high-scoring Game 1, Monday’s rematch ended in a 1-1 regulation tie. Kosta Likourezos scored the equalizer for The Sahara late in the third period, forcing an OT/Shootout. By securing the tie and the extra point, The Sahara advanced with 3 series points. **The 4 Lines roster looked completely lazy and checked-out during the final minutes, seemingly content to let their season end without a fight.**

**The Shockers (3) vs Flat-Earthers (4): The Comeback**
The Shockers punched their ticket to the Semi-Finals with a 2-1 regulation win on Monday. Trailing 1-0 entering the third, Keelan Hughes tied the game before Patrick Mcmullan netted the series-clinching goal at 05:46. The Shockers advance with 3 series points.

#### The Lucky Loser Math
Despite the loss, the **Flat-Earthers** advance to the Semi-Finals. With 1 series point and a -1 goal differential, they edged out 4 Lines (-2) and the Muffin Men (-10) to secure the final bracket spot.

#### Three Stars of the Week
1. **Michael Murphy (Don Cherry's):** 2 Goals, 1 Assist in the clincher.
2. **Patrick Mcmullan (The Shockers):** Scored the game-winning goal to advance.
3. **Kosta Likourezos (The Sahara):** Scored the crucial tying goal to force OT.
